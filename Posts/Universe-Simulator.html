Universe Simulator - project overview and plans

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Universe Simulator is my personal project. It's space sandbox, with some logic features. Honestly I wanted to make game like Poly Bridge but in space. It's hosted on Github

Features

I've added most of core features to the game. The most important one - level editor with level saving and loading is here. Latest feature that I've been working on is logic in levels.

Why did I abandon working on the project, and what you can do with it?

Because of recent Unity's moves, I decided to abandon project. It was very painful to watch what company can do with their tool, with their customers and with hobbyists. However feel free to make something from Universe Simulator. Go ahead, fork and play with it :)
