about me

I'm a computer science student at the University of Technology in Wrocław (WUST). I'm a technology enthusiast who enjoys solving problems, which I often encounter while programming. Outside of my studies, I create video games and work on various interesting projects, many of which I host on my GitHub. I also contribute to engaging open-source initiatives.
